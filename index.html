Project Info
About

MeetMeFirst is an AI-powered pre-introduction platform for executive personal branding. Domain-filtered profiles with AI clone representatives let high-profile individuals control their first impressions.

Who It's For
C-Suite Execs Board Members VCs & Founders Public Figures HNW Individuals
Scenario

An executive needs to be introduced at a private dinner, a board event, or a dating context. Instead of a LinkedIn profile or cold intro, their AI clone provides a curated, context-aware introduction filtered for the specific domain.

Flow
1

Executive creates a profile on MeetMeFirst

2

Trains their AI clone with voice, tone, and boundaries

3

Configures domain filters (career, social, dating, etc.)

4

Shares a unique introduction link or QR code

5

Recipient interacts with the AI clone for a curated intro

6

Executive reviews interaction transcript and decides next step

7

Mutual interest triggers a warm introduction
